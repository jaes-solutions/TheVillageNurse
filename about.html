Our Story

At The Village Nurse Ltd, we believe care should go beyond tasks it should bring comfort, dignity, and peace of mind.

Founded by Omonigho Vikky Etokakpan, a highly experienced nurse with over 16 years in healthcare, The Village Nurse was created to bridge the gap between hospital care and everyday living.
